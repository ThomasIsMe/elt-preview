News

What ELT has been up to.

25 February

ELT launches $660m inputs scheme

Ethical Leaf Tobacco unveiled a $660 million input facility, the Gold Class Scheme, split into four tiers: Gold for 1,000kg and above, Diamond 700kg, Silver 500kg and Bronze under 500kg.

01 September

ELT donates PPE to clinics around the country

As part of its community engagement, ELT donated personal protective equipment to clinics across Zimbabwe, backing the health workers who serve the farming communities it finances.

24 November

ELT decentralises for farmer convenience

Ethical Leaf Tobacco moved services closer to the growers, decentralising operations so farmers spend less time travelling and more time on the crop.
