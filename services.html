Bridging finance, seed bed to floor.

ELT gives smallholder tobacco farmers everything a season needs before the season starts: inputs, capital, agronomy and a guaranteed route to the auction floor. Ten services, one unbroken line of support.

What ELT carries

Giving bridging finance to smallholder farmers, in every form a tobacco season demands. Hover a card to read what it covers.

01

Seed bed package

We support the farmer from the onset of the season with a quality seed bed package. The seedbed sets the pace for the quality of the whole crop.

02

Land pack package

Recommended tobacco chemicals and fertilizers, plus the working capital to put them in the ground.

03

Chemicals & fertilizers

A range of effective tobacco chemicals (Imidacloprid, Tide, Bullet, Decanol) and fertilizers, delivered to the farm gate.

04

Tobacco packaging

Hessian and paper packaging to preserve the cured leaf and protect its grade on the way to the floor.

05

Working capital

Provision of all working capital requirements, so a grower with no upfront cash can still plant a full season.

06

Extension services

Training and agronomy guidance for smallholder farmers, part of ELT's non-monetary support. The farmer is never left to guess.

07

Tobacco marketing

Our mantra: Kutengesa neHungwaru, selling with excellence. Every financed crop reaches the floor and settles fairly.

08

Logistics

Our logistics team ensures farmers receive their inputs on time and have a smooth tobacco marketing season.

09

Microinsurance

We have the back of our smallholder farmers covered against the risk of unforeseen circumstances.

10

Gold Class scheme

The $660m flagship: a four-tier input facility (Gold, Diamond, Silver, Bronze) scaled to what each farmer produces.
