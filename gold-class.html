Four tiers, one scheme

Every tier carries the farmer; the package scales with the crop. Tiers are set by ELT's own published thresholds.

Gold
1,000 kg and above

The full package: seed bed, land pack, curing fuel, packaging, priority logistics, a dedicated extension officer and microinsurance.

Diamond
700 kg

Seed bed, land pack, working capital, extension support and microinsurance cover for a strong commercial season.

Silver
500 kg

Seed bed, core chemicals and fertilizer, working capital and on-the-ground extension support.

Bronze
Under 500 kg

A starter package: seed bed, essential inputs and extension support to build a first or smaller season.
